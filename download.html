AbChess v0.1.0

Initial release. Date : 1 Mar 2016

Main features

  • Draw a chessboard.
  • Flip the chessboard.
  • Play a move :
    • By clicking.
    • By dragging.
    • Programmatically.
  • Squares highlighting :
    • King in check.
    • Last move.
    • Legal move possibilities.
    • Overflown square.
    • Selected piece.
  • Test legal chess moves.
  • Export a position.
  • Import a position.
  • Navigate in a game.
  • Export a game.
  • Import a game.
